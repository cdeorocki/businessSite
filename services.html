Provided Services

Day Visits

Day visits with your dog(s) include a walk, a wipe down if they get wet, a report card detailing their day, food if requested, fresh water and cookies.

Day visits with your cat(s) include rubbing them down, playing with them, changing their litter, a report card detailing their day, food if requested, fresh water and cookies.

I try to work your pet's day visit around a time that best suits you both. Depending on the hours you are away, we can set up a time frame that would be most beneficial to your pet.

Stay-cations

During a stay-cation I will stay with your furry pal at your house each night while you are away. This is often a much prefered option to kennel boarding or periodic visits.

Dinner visits and morning walks are included in the stay-cation. I may also be able to provide private boarding for your cat at my home, although this would depend on availability as I am only willing to keep one client's pet(s) at my place at a time.

I want to keep your furry friend company! I call it a "stay-cation" because I promise that your pet will be having a mini vacation of their own, even while you are away.
